Design a premium, board-level pricing section for a healthcare AI governance advisory website.

The positioning is serious, calm, analytical and executive-level. 
Avoid startup, techy, flashy or SaaS-style pricing cards.

This is NOT a subscription product.
This is a fixed-scope strategic assessment for healthcare institutions.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION GOAL
--------------------------------------------------

Communicate:

AI Governance Assessment
Fixed trajectory price between €4.000 – €6.000
Clear scope
High trust
No “starting from” phrasing
No aggressive sales tone
No startup aesthetics

The section must feel:
• Medical-sector appropriate
• Governance-oriented
• Calm and controlled
• Structured
• Executive-ready

--------------------------------------------------
LAYOUT STRUCTURE
--------------------------------------------------

1. Section label (small capsule style, subtle)
   Text:
   “INVESTERING”

2. Headline (large, strong typography)
   “AI Governance Assessment”

3. Subheadline (medium weight, calm tone)
   “Een zes weken durend traject voor bestuurlijke grip op AI-gebruik binnen zorgorganisaties.”

4. Pricing block (centered, strong but not flashy)

   Main line:
   “Vaste trajectprijs: €4.000 – €6.000”

   Subtext:
   “De exacte prijs wordt bepaald op basis van omvang, aantal AI-systemen en complexiteit van het leverancierslandschap.”

   Styling:
   - No gradient
   - No colorful pricing badges
   - No discount styling
   - Dark typography on light background
   - Strong spacing
   - Executive look

5. Scope breakdown (two-column layout)

Left column title:
“Wat is inbegrepen”

Bullet points:
• Volledige AI-inventarisatie
• Governance- en compliance-analyse (AVG / EU AI Act / NIS2)
• Risicomatrix en scenariogebaseerde financiële inschatting
• Concrete bestuursbesluiten en prioritering
• Boardpresentatie

Right column title:
“Wanneer €4.000 of €6.000?”

Short structured explanation:

Lower range:
“Kleinere zorginstellingen met beperkt AI-landschap (≤10 systemen).”

Upper range:
“Grotere organisaties met meerdere locaties of complex leverancierslandschap.”

Tone: calm, factual, not sales-driven.

6. Optional closing reassurance block (subtle box, light background)

Text:

“Transparantie en voorspelbaarheid staan centraal. 
De prijs wordt vooraf vastgesteld na een korte scopebepaling. 
Er zijn geen variabele nacalculaties.”

--------------------------------------------------
VISUAL STYLE RULES
--------------------------------------------------

• Use the same typography as the rest of the website.
• No startup-style pricing cards.
• No shadows that feel SaaS-like.
• Minimal rounded corners.
• Muted healthcare-appropriate color palette (navy, slate, subtle blue accents).
• Plenty of white space.
• Structured grid alignment.
• Executive-level hierarchy.

Avoid:
- Gradient backgrounds
- “Most popular” badges
- Animated counters
- Neon or tech startup visuals
- Overly rounded UI elements
- Sales urgency language

This must look like:
A serious advisory firm serving hospital boards.
