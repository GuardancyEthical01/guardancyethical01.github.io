You are a senior brand designer specialized in healthcare advisory firms, medical compliance consultancies and regulatory institutions.

My current website feels like a generic AI SaaS landing page:
- Too many rounded cards
- Too many blue outline icons
- Repetitive 3-column grids
- Too “startup AI tool”
- Too much visual decoration
- Not enough authority

The website is for a medical AI governance advisory firm serving:
- Hospital boards
- Compliance officers
- FG/DPO roles
- Medical professionals

Your task: Redesign the visual system so it feels like a serious healthcare governance advisory firm — not a tech startup.

--------------------------------------------------
POSITIONING
--------------------------------------------------

This is NOT:
- A SaaS product
- A startup
- A productivity tool
- A tech platform

This IS:
- Independent advisory
- Governance & compliance
- Medical sector expertise
- Risk assessment & policy guidance

The tone must feel:
- Calm
- Clinical
- Structured
- Precise
- Trustworthy

--------------------------------------------------
VISUAL PRINCIPLES
--------------------------------------------------

1. Typography-first design
2. Remove decorative UI patterns
3. Reduce icon usage drastically
4. Avoid SaaS-style pricing cards
5. Use white space intentionally
6. No playful badges or marketing highlights
7. No “most chosen” tags

--------------------------------------------------
COLOR SYSTEM
--------------------------------------------------

Primary: Deep neutral (almost black)
Accent: Muted medical blue (not bright tech blue)
Optional secondary: Subtle sage/medical green (very restrained)

No gradients.
No glowing buttons.
No vibrant startup colors.

--------------------------------------------------
LAYOUT TRANSFORMATION
--------------------------------------------------

Replace repetitive rounded cards with:

- Clean sections separated by whitespace
- Thin horizontal dividers
- Structured text blocks
- Numbered methodology sections
- Editorial-style layout

Example transformation:

Instead of:

[icon] Risico- en complianceanalyse
(card with bullets)

Use:

02
Risico- en complianceanalyse

AVG-toets en uitvoering DPIA  
AI Act impactanalyse  
Analyse van datastromen  
Leveranciers- en ketenonderzoek  

No icon.
No rounded background.
Just structured, confident typography.

--------------------------------------------------
PRICING TRANSFORMATION
--------------------------------------------------

Remove SaaS-style pricing cards completely.

Reformat into a medical advisory format:

Safe Start  
Vanaf €3.000  

Een eenmalige governance-analyse voor één afgebakende AI-toepassing binnen uw organisatie.

Looptijd: 4–6 weken  
Resultaat: audit-geschikte documentatie en bestuurlijke besluitvorming

Subtle CTA button below.
No visual highlight tricks.
No borders in accent color.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION STYLE
--------------------------------------------------

Use section labels in small uppercase:
“WERKWIJZE”
“DELIVERABLES”
“RISICO-INDICATOREN”
“VOOR WIE”

Large, calm headings.
Generous spacing.
Less visual repetition.

--------------------------------------------------
MEDICAL AUTHORITY SIGNALS
--------------------------------------------------

Design should subtly communicate:

- Familiarity with AVG, DPIA, AI Act
- Healthcare governance
- Audit and supervisory context
- Institutional seriousness

It should feel like something a hospital board would confidently review.

--------------------------------------------------
FINAL TEST

After redesign, evaluate:

Does this feel like a healthcare governance advisory firm?
Would a medical compliance officer trust this?
Does this feel calm and serious?
Does it avoid all startup SaaS aesthetics?

If not, simplify further.
Remove decoration.
Increase typographic clarity.
